Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Agenda ("we", "our", "the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our calendar application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (used for authentication and account recovery)
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)
  • Username (optional, chosen by you)

Calendar Data

When you use the Service, we store:

  • Event titles, descriptions, dates, and times
  • Event categories and color preferences
  • Reminder settings
  • Shared event data (emails of users you share events with)

Google Calendar Data

If you choose to connect Google Calendar, we access your Google Calendar events to display them within the Service. This connection is optional and can be disconnected at any time.

AI Chat Data

When you use the AI chat feature, your messages are processed to provide scheduling assistance. Chat history is stored locally in your browser session and is not permanently retained on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Store and display your calendar events
  • Send reminders and notifications you have configured
  • Provide AI-powered scheduling suggestions based on your events
  • Improve the Service's functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using AWS cloud infrastructure (AWS Amplify and related services). We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing via AWS Cognito
  • Access controls to limit data access

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these cases:

  • With other users: When you explicitly share an event with someone
  • Service providers: AWS (infrastructure), Google (if you connect Google Calendar)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the application interface
  • Edit or delete your events at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect third-party integrations (Google Calendar)
  • Change your notification and reminder preferences

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Event history is automatically cleared after 7 days. If you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

The Service uses browser session storage to maintain your login session and store preferences (such as theme and language). We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the Service. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out through the application's support channels.
