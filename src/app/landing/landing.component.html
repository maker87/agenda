Frequently asked questions

Is Agenda free to use?

Yes! Agenda offers a generous free tier that includes all core features like event management, categories, smart reminders, and AI scheduling suggestions.

Can I import events from Google Calendar?

Absolutely. Connect your Google account and Agenda will sync your events automatically. Changes made in either app stay up to date.

How does the AI scheduling work?

Our AI analyzes your existing schedule, habits, and preferences to suggest optimal times for new events. It considers factors like meeting gaps, focus time, and your personal routines.

What can I do with the AI chat?

You can ask natural-language questions like "When am I free tomorrow?" or give commands like "Schedule a workout at 7 AM on weekdays." The AI understands context and can manage your schedule conversationally.

Is my data safe?

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use AWS infrastructure with strict access controls. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.

Can I use Agenda on mobile?

Agenda is built as a responsive web app that works beautifully on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. No app download needed.

Can I share events with other people?

Yes! You can share individual events or entire calendars with anyone. They'll receive an invite notification and can accept or decline right from their notification panel.

Does it support dark mode?

Of course. Toggle between light and dark themes with a single click from the dashboard. Your preference is saved automatically.
